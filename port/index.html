HI! I'M NIKLAS.
BUT YOU CAN CALL ME ANDY.

I am a filmmaker, editor, motion and graphic designer from Salzburg, Austria. Fueled by my perfectionist drive, I consistently push the envelope to deliver extraordinary results that leave a lasting impact.

From 2017 to 2022 I studied Multimedia at the HTBLuVA in Salzburg. I've graduated in July of 2022 and passed my finals with distinction with a diploma in Multimedia and Interaction Design.

Not only am I a passionate creative but I am also a professional freestyle trampoline athlete. I combine both my talents in videography and sports by showcasing them on my instagram and YouTube channel. This dynamic union has opened many doors for me to collaborations with big name clients across the globe, carving a niche where innovation and imagination thrive.
