NIKLAS DISTLER

Radial Flipz 9 Series

RadialFlipz is the worlds leading Freestyle Trampoline Team, blending professionalism with captivating and enjoyable content.

In 2022 their Team Member Ernest Brenchley managed to break the world record of the most consecutive flips on a trampoline with a nonuple kaboom (9 somersaults).

In 2023, RadialFlipz collaborated with me again to design their annual Summer merch drop.

This year they asked for a streetwear design, that was suitable for any semi- casual occasion, yet still incorporating their brand identity.

Inspired by their recently accomplished world record of a nonuple kaboom I decided to create a collection revolving around the number 9.
