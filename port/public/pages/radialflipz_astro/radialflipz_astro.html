RadialFlipz Merch 2022

RadialFlipz is the worlds leading Freestyle Trampoline Team, blending professionalism with captivating and enjoyable content. They challenge the notion that Freestyle Trampoline allows self- expression without the rigidity of Gymnastics Trampoline. The team holds several world records, notably a nonuple kaboom (9 Somersaults) and the highest jump at 13 meters.

In 2022, RadialFlipz collaborated with me to design their annual Summer merch drop.
